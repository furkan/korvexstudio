Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 05, 2026

Welcome to Twin Tails. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information Collection

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or phone number. Twin Tails is designed to be a safe, offline-first experience.

2. Gameplay Data

Game progress, stars earned, and unlocked items are stored locally on your device. We do not transmit this data to our servers unless you explicitly use a cloud-sync feature (if available in future updates).

3. Analytics

We may use anonymous analytics tools (like Unity Analytics or Firebase) to understand how players interact with the game. This data helps us improve level design and fix bugs. No personal data is included in these reports.

4. Advertising

Twin Tails may show advertisements to support development. These ad networks may use device identifiers to serve relevant ads. You can opt-out of personalized advertising through your device settings.

5. Children's Privacy

Twin Tails is a family-friendly game. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
