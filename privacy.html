Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

Welcome to our game studio. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile applications, specifically Twin Tails and Plate Spin.

1. Information Collection

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, physical address, or phone number. Our games are designed to provide a fun experience without requiring user registration or account creation.

2. Gameplay Data

Your game progress (such as levels completed, stars earned, high scores, and unlocked items) is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit this data to our servers. Please note that if you delete the app or clear your device data, your progress may be lost as we do not maintain cloud backups of your personal gameplay.

3. Analytics

To help us improve our games, fix bugs, and optimize level difficulty, we use anonymous analytics tools:

  • Plate Spin: Uses GameAnalytics to track anonymous events like level start/end times and performance metrics.
  • Twin Tails: May use Unity Analytics or Firebase for similar anonymous performance tracking.

The data collected is purely technical and cannot be used to identify you personally.

4. Advertising

Our games may show advertisements to support ongoing development.
Specifically, Plate Spin integrates AdMob to serve advertisements. Ad networks may use device identifiers (such as the Android Advertising ID or iOS IDFA) to serve relevant ads. You can manage or opt-out of personalized advertising through your mobile device's privacy settings.

5. Children's Privacy

Both Twin Tails and Plate Spin are intended to be family-friendly. We do not knowingly collect, request, or track any personal information from children under the age of 13. Our third-party partners (Analytics and Ads) are configured to comply with child safety regulations (such as COPPA).

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our games or legal requirements. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
